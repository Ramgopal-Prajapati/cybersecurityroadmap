Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about our Cybersecurity & Ethical Hacking program.

Do I need prior IT experience for this cybersecurity course? +
Basic computer knowledge is sufficient. We start from networking fundamentals and gradually progress to advanced security topics. The course is designed for beginners as well as IT professionals wanting to switch to cybersecurity.
What certifications will I prepare for during the course? +
You'll prepare for CEH (Certified Ethical Hacker) and CompTIA Security+ certifications. Course includes exam preparation materials, practice tests, and guidance for additional certifications like CCNA Security and OSCP.
Will I get hands-on practice with security tools? +
Absolutely! You'll work with Kali Linux, Metasploit, Burp Suite, Wireshark, Nmap, John the Ripper, and other industry-standard tools in our virtual labs. We provide access to vulnerable machines and CTF platforms for practice.
Is there any placement assistance? +
Yes! We provide complete placement assistance including resume building with security projects, LinkedIn profile optimization, mock interviews, and direct recruitment with our partner companies in cybersecurity domain.
What is the course duration and schedule? +
The program is 4 months long with 2-hour classes from Monday to Friday. Weekend batches available. Recordings provided for missed classes. Daily lab exercises and weekly security projects ensure practical learning.
